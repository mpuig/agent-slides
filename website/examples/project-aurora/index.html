Example — 10 content slides

Product launch strategy.

A mid-size strategy deck for Project Aurora, an AI-powered customer service platform. Covers market sizing, competitive analysis, revenue model, and three-phase go-to-market plan.

The prompt

Create a 10-slide product launch strategy deck
on "Project Aurora: AI-Powered Customer Service
Platform" using /slides-full.

Audience: Executive Leadership Team and Product Board.
Tone: Strategic pitch (vision + evidence, projected).

Scope: Market sizing ($31B by 2027), three unmet
customer needs, product differentiation (sentiment
analysis + autonomous resolution), competitive feature
comparison, subscription revenue model, three-phase
go-to-market plan, Year 1 targets ($18M ARR,
200 accounts).

Use section dividers between Market Opportunity and
Product & Differentiation, a bar chart for market
growth, a pie chart for revenue mix, a feature
comparison table, icon-grid cards for capabilities,
and a process flow for the GTM phases. Add speaker
notes on the exec summary and recommendation slides.

Source: Microsoft free template gallery — a geometric 16:9 template with clean lines and a professional layout.

Reproduce it

# 1. Download the template from Microsoft

# 2. Extract
/slides-extract example2.pptx

# 3. Build (paste the prompt above)
/slides-full "10-slide product launch strategy ..."

# Or render from the JSON directly
slides render \
  --slides-json @slides.json \
  --profile design-profile.json \
  --output output.pptx
