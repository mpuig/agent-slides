Example — 15 content slides

Southeast Asia market expansion.

A comprehensive investment case for a $45M phased market entry targeting Indonesia, Vietnam, and Thailand. Three sections, four charts, two tables, and scenario analysis.

The prompt

Create a 15-slide market expansion strategy deck
on "Southeast Asia Market Entry" for the Executive
Committee using /slides-full.

Audience: Executive Committee and Board of Directors.
Tone: Formal investment case (data-heavy,
recommendation-driven).

Scope: SEA digital economy overview ($218B), SaaS
adoption trends, top 3 markets (Indonesia, Vietnam,
Thailand = 78% of TAM), competitive fragmentation
analysis, platform differentiation in localization
and compliance, revenue ramp to $67M by Year 3,
IRR 38%, 2.4-year payback, four-phase rollout plan,
three-scenario NPV analysis, $45M investment
recommendation.

Structure with three sections (Market Landscape,
Competitive Analysis, Financial Case). Use two bar
charts, a line chart, and a pie chart. Include two
data tables (competitor landscape + scenario comparison),
three-column country profiles with icons, big-number
KPI cards, a process flow for the rollout phases,
and source lines on all data slides. Add speaker notes
on the exec summary, KPI, and recommendation slides.

Template

Source: Microsoft free template gallery — a presentation-skills themed 16:9 template with 12 layouts, photo-driven section headers, and a dark overlay aesthetic.

Deck structure

The deck is organized into three sections with clear narrative progression:

Opening

  1. Title slide
    Southeast Asia Market Entry
  2. Executive summary
    A $45M investment in Southeast Asia will generate $67M annual revenue by Year 3 with 38% IRR

Section 1: Market Landscape

  1. Section divider
  2. Bar chart — regional comparison
    SEA's digital economy reached $218B in 2024 and is projected to exceed $360B by 2027
  3. Line chart — SaaS adoption trend
    Enterprise SaaS adoption in SEA is accelerating at 34% CAGR, outpacing global averages
  4. Three-column — country profiles
    Indonesia, Vietnam, and Thailand represent 78% of SEA's total addressable market

Section 2: Competitive Analysis

  1. Section divider
  2. Pie chart — market share fragmentation
    No single player holds more than 12% market share, signaling high competitive fragmentation
  3. Table — competitor comparison
    Local competitors lack enterprise-grade compliance; global players underinvest in localization
  4. Two-column — differentiation
    Our platform differentiates through deep localization and regulatory compliance capabilities

Section 3: Financial Case

  1. Section divider
  2. Big number — IRR and payback
    The investment delivers 38% IRR with a 2.4-year payback period
  3. Bar chart — revenue ramp
    Revenue ramps from $8M in Year 1 to $67M by Year 3 across three markets
  4. Table — scenario analysis
    Even the bear case delivers positive NPV, confirming downside protection
  5. Process flow — rollout phases
    A four-phase rollout de-risks execution with clear stage gates
  6. Recommendation
    We recommend approving $45M to capture SEA's high-growth SaaS opportunity

Closing

  1. Disclaimer
  2. End slide

Reproduce it

# 1. Download the template from Microsoft

# 2. Extract
/slides-extract example3.pptx

# 3. Build (paste the prompt above)
/slides-full "15-slide market expansion strategy ..."

# Or render from the JSON directly
slides render \
  --slides-json @slides.json \
  --profile design-profile.json \
  --output output.pptx
